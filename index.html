About Us

Hello,
We are a team of physical therapy Group (B) students at BUC.

We are passionate about the rehabilitation of ACL injuries.
Our goal is to provide helpful resources and support for those affected by ACL injuries through education and specialized rehabilitation techniques:

“Rehabilitation of Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) Injury.”

About our Project

This project aims to raise awareness about Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) injuries. It Provides downloadable educational resources and instructional videos covering diagnosis, surgery, and rehabilitation techniques. It's useful for students, athletes, and physical therapy professionals.
